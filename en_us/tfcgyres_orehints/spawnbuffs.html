Better Ore Veins

Making hint rocks also includes the mineral veins themselves, so why not make them better? Some of the veins were too small and too rare, so sizes, rarities, and some shapes changed!

Coal veins are now much larger horizontal discs with fewer inclusions. Most other mineral veins are more common and doubled in radius. Y-level restrictions are the same.

Deep Ore Veins

Exploring the depths of the world may be more rewarding! More veins below y-level 0 now exist for all metamorphic and igneous intrusive ores: metal, mineral, and diamonds/emeralds. These veins may be larger, denser, and purer.

There are no iron ores that spawn deep as sedimentary and igneous extrusive rocks do not spawn on the bottom layer.
